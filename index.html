Brand Story

The word muse does not belong to the artist alone.

Muse by Anahita was founded on a simple conviction: that the act of being seen — truly seen — is transformative. Each client who sits in the chair is not a canvas to be worked on, but a collaborator.

The studio serves brides, editorial clients, and everyday visitors seeking moments of extraordinary care. The approach is the same in all cases — unhurried, attentive, and deeply personal.
